The problem

Recurring droughts in Namibia are causing severe water shortages and food insecurity. Smallholder farming communities are hit hardest. Farmers lack the actionable climate data needed to prepare or respond.

Our goal

Delivering practical climate recommendations and water management tools. Boosting long-term food security and climate resilience across Namibia.
